Our Story

ToolsKit was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, free online tools for working with images and PDFs. We recognized that many people needed access to these tools but couldn't afford expensive software or didn't want to install applications on their devices.

What started as a small collection of basic tools has grown into a comprehensive platform serving millions of users worldwide. Our team is passionate about creating tools that are not only powerful but also easy to use for everyone, regardless of their technical skills.

We believe in keeping our tools free and accessible to all. Our business model relies on non-intrusive advertising and optional premium features that help support the development and maintenance of our platform.

Our Mission

At ToolsKit, we're committed to making file conversion and optimization as simple and efficient as possible. We understand that our users rely on our tools for important work, education, and personal projects, so we prioritize reliability and quality in everything we do.

Our mission goes beyond just providing tools. We aim to educate our users about file formats, optimization techniques, and best practices through our blog and tutorials. We believe that by empowering users with knowledge and tools, we can help them work more effectively in our increasingly digital world.

Privacy is at the core of our mission. Unlike many other online tools, we process all files directly in your browser whenever possible, ensuring your sensitive documents never touch our servers.
